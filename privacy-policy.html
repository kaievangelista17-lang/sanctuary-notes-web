Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Sanctuary Notes is built for the church. We believe your personal notes, reflections, and prayers are sacred — and we take your privacy seriously. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.

Information We Collect

Sanctuary Notes stores all your notes, drawings, and settings locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers.

Bible API Services

When you search for Bible passages or load the Verse of the Day, your search query is sent to third-party Bible APIs to retrieve scripture content. These requests contain no personal identifying information — only the scripture reference or keyword you searched for.

Services used:

  • YouVersion Bible API — for Verse of the Day and passage lookup
  • API.Bible — for keyword Bible search across multiple translations

Data Storage

All notes, drawings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure AsyncStorage API. Your data does not leave your device except for the Bible search queries described above.

Apple Pencil and Drawing Data

Drawing and handwriting data created with Apple Pencil is stored locally on your device as part of your notes. We do not access, transmit, or process your drawing data in any way.

Analytics and Tracking

Sanctuary Notes does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services. We do not track your usage patterns, behavior, device information, or any other activity within the app.

Advertising

Sanctuary Notes contains no advertising of any kind. We do not work with ad networks and do not share any data with advertisers.

In-App Purchases

Sanctuary Notes does not currently offer in-app purchases. Any future purchases will be processed securely through Apple's App Store and subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Sanctuary Notes does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no advertising, social features, or data collection of any kind, making it safe for all ages.

Third-Party Links

Sanctuary Notes may display links to third-party websites such as Bible reference sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their policies.

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, your notes are protected by your device's built-in security features including Face ID, Touch ID, and device encryption.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the app grows. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete or export on your behalf. You can delete all your notes and data by uninstalling the app from your device.

Questions about privacy?

We're happy to answer any questions you have about how Sanctuary Notes handles your data.
