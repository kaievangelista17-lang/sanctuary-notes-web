Common Questions

How can we help?

How do I switch between Sermon, Study, Journal, and Prayer tabs?

Open any note and tap the tab pills at the top of the editor — Sermon, Study, Journal, or Prayer. Each tab saves its content independently, so you can freely switch without losing anything.

How do I search the Bible and insert a verse into my note?

Tap the ✦ Bible button in the note toolbar. Search by reference (e.g. "John 3:16") or keyword (e.g. "faith"). Once you find your verse, tap "+ Insert into Note" to add it directly to your current note.

How do I use Apple Pencil to draw in my notes?

Tap the ✐ Draw button in the toolbar to enter Draw mode. Your Apple Pencil will draw on the canvas. Use your finger to scroll. Switch back to Type mode to continue typing — your drawings stay visible underneath.

Are my notes backed up?

Currently, notes are stored locally on your device. We recommend regularly exporting important notes as PDFs as a backup. Cloud sync is coming in a future update.

How do I change the app theme?

Tap the theme button (the colored dot) in the top right of the home screen. Choose from Parchment, Warm Classic, Dark Reverent, Midnight Blue, or Clean Minimal.

How do I export a note as PDF?

Open a note, tap the ••• menu in the top right, then tap "Share as PDF". Your note will be formatted and ready to share via AirDrop, email, or any other app.

How do I import notes from Apple Notes or Notability?

Export your note from Apple Notes or Notability to the Files app first, then open Sanctuary Notes, go to Import, and tap "Choose File to Import". We support TXT, PDF, RTF, and more.

Can I use Sanctuary Notes without an internet connection?

Yes — all your notes are stored locally and work fully offline. Bible search and the Verse of the Day require an internet connection, but everything else works without one.

How do I delete multiple notes at once?

On the home screen, tap "✦ Select" in the header to enter select mode. Tap the notes you want to select, then tap "Delete" in the action bar that appears at the bottom.

What translations are available for Bible search?

Sanctuary Notes supports NASB 2020, NIV, ESV, KJV, NLT, CSB, and NKJV. You can switch translations in the Bible Search screen or in Settings.
